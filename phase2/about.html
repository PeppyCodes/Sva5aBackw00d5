About Us

Svasa is based on South Karnataka, in a move to support the rural and the local farmers. Svasa is a brainchild of Nikita, a passionate Entrepreneur. In a world of adulteration and unhealthy products, her mission is to create a healthier future.

Our Mission

Through our healthy organic products, we want to create a healthier future for generations to come and We combine traditional methods with modern standards for best organic products.

Our Promise

We will never compromise on quality or standards and we will always provide unadulterated products.

Why SVASA ?

All the products of SVASA support health and True wellness and are made with loving care. Each product is one link in a chain of connectedness between mother nature, our farmers and you.

Our Values
  • Organic products have come a long way, since its inception. It is not just about the organic products that we sell, but also the service along with value-added services that we offer you.
  • Being in a market where the competition is cutthroat, there have been many challenges in providing absolutely chemical free products.
Our History

Hifi Network Hub is the basic platform where SVASA started. Positive feedback and consequently high demand, made SVASA contemplate taking our efforts and introducing it to the people. We started selling organic products which are grown by local farmers. And people instantaneously started to tell the difference. The local communities are now choosing our premium organic products over the other options available to them, and we have realized that our dream of providing pure and absolutely organic products is definitely in demand.

How we work

Eat & be happy

  • At the heart of SVASA I sour commitment to be a living an embodiment of love and consciousness in action
  • We work with a few family farmers to produce organic and natural products.
  • What sets us a level above our competitors is the dedicated workforce right from the grass root level till secondary services.
  • We utilize most of our resources on various levels of quality control measures. Right from the farm level, our graduation and sorting begin.
  • Under completely hygienic conditions our the goods are packed and made available to you.
